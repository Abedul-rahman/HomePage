JU Halls Services

Welcome to JU Halls, your dedicated platform for scheduling and reserving university halls with Simplicity.
Our goal is to offer you an easy and effective way of managing hall bookings at the University of Jordan for lectures, events, and study groups.
The primary services we offer:

Real-Time Hall Availability

You can view up to date schedules for all university halls, ensuring transparency and efficiency in bookings.

Customized Hall Preferences

You can filter halls based on capacity, equipment (e.g., projectors, audio systems), and location within the university.

Booking History and Management

You can to view and manage their past and upcoming bookings, including the ability to modify or cancel reservations.

About Us

Who We Are
